§ 03 · Protocol

Four steps.
No magic.
No demo
theater.

Every action is a written instruction, every instruction has a paper trail. If the agent can't show its receipts, the agent doesn't ship.

See the spec
  1. 01

    Hand it the keys

    Connect your inbox, calendar, accounting, and document store. Read-only by default. Write access is opt-in, per surface.

    5 min
  2. 02

    Teach it your house style

    Paste three past emails, one redlined contract, one Slack post. That's the voice. It writes the way you write, or doesn't write at all.

    DAY ONE
  3. 03

    Let it run, in dry mode

    For a week, it drafts everything and sends nothing. You approve, edit, or kill. After 200 approvals on a surface, the surface goes live.

    SHADOW
  4. 04

    Get your afternoons back

    The boring half is done. The interesting half is yours.

§ 04 · Specification

Built like an instrument, not a chatbot.

Deterministic where it can be, accountable where it can't. The agent prefers a small set of well-formed tools over a sprawl of plug-ins. Every decision lives in an append-only log you can read at 2am.

  • —— Self-hostable / on your VPC
  • —— Read-only by default, write per-surface
  • —— SOC 2 type II · ISO 27001 · GDPR
  • —— Models swappable: keep your own
  • —— Every action exportable as PDF receipt
