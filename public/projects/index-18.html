The lineup

Three flavors. That’s the whole menu.

We pick three citrus varieties a season, press them cold, and bottle them with sparkling water from a single Portuguese source. That’s it.

01 / Peel

Bitter Mandarin

Hand-pressed Setubal mandarins with their peel still on. Dry finish, faint marmalade hum, the kind of bitter you actually want. Best at 4°C · Pairs with salty almonds

02 / Cherry

Blood Orange

Sicilian Moro oranges, the deep-purple kind that stain everything they touch. Tart up front, sweet on the back, no sugar added at any step. Best at 4°C · Pairs with a salt rim and mezcal

03 / Grove

Yuzu & Lime

Japanese yuzu blended with Persian lime. Aggressive, sharp, a little floral. The bottle that makes everything else taste flat. Best at 4°C · Pairs with raw fish and bad days

We started Pith because the soda aisle had become a chemistry set. Ours has three ingredients: water, fruit, fizz. If a kid can’t read the back label, we don’t bottle it.

— Marta & João, founders Lisbon · Small batch · Glass only
