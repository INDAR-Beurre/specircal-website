The Method · 01

Drawn in pencil.
Forced into pixels.

Every Rg face starts in a notebook, gets roughed up in Glyphs, then tested across the kind of spreads nobody reads on purpose. We don't ship until the kerning stops arguing with us.

Software later.

Roughs go on paper, then get refined at 400% zoom until the counter-shapes feel right. Vectorizing is the last step, not the first.
