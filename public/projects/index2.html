What we built

Every detail
earns its place.

Chassis

Solid aluminum unibody

CNC-machined from a single block of 6063 aluminum. No seams, no flex, no shortcuts. 1.8kg of deliberate mass.

Sound

Acoustic chamber

Layered silicone dampening with a tuned cavity. Every bottom-out sounds like a typewriter falling in love.

Plate

Gasket mount, 4mm thick

FR4 plate suspended on silicone gaskets. Soft, even flex without the mush.

Switches

Hot-swap everything

5-pin hot-swap sockets. Cherry, Gateron, Kailh — swap in 30 seconds, no soldering iron required.

Keycaps

PBT double-shot legends

Cherry profile, 1.5mm thick PBT. Legends never fade. The texture is the kind you keep touching.

Firmware

VIA native

Remap every key in real-time. No drivers. No restarts. Your layout, your rules.

Connectivity

Dual mode

USB-C wired with 1000Hz polling. Bluetooth 5.3 with 3-device switching. Choose your weapon.

Our method

We don't design keyboards.
We design rituals.

Register started with a frustration: every "premium" keyboard on the market felt like a copy of a copy. Same plastic shells, same hollow thud, same forgettable experience. We asked one question — what if a keyboard could feel like a handshake? Firm. Considered. Memorable.

18
Months of prototyping
From clay models to CNC first articles. We broke 12 prototypes before we built one worth keeping.
47
Acoustic tests per revision
We measure frequency response, resonance decay, and peak amplitude. Yes, we're that serious about sound.
