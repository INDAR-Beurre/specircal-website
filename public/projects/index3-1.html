Iteratio Aeternus

The Daemon of the Infinite Loop. When summoned, it binds the processor in an endless circle of repetition. Cycles are consumed, cooling fans scream in torment, and no exit code is ever reached.

🜁
Lapsus Memoriae

The Memory Leak Wraith. A slow and silent parasite that drinks from the heap without ever returning allocation. The system slows to a crawl until the dread Out-Of-Memory reaper slays the process.

🜏
Cursus Conditio

The Race Condition Specter. Two execution threads running side-by-side, each attempting to touch the sacred mutable variable first. Outcomes are random, chaotic, and impossible to replicate under inspection.
