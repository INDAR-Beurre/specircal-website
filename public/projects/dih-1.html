THE RADICAL ETHOS

No decoration. No compromise. Only geometry.

"We reject the temporary. We do not build for comfort or decoration. We build monolithic concrete objects designed to survive the next geological epoch."

Architectural design has become fragile and obsessed with impermanence. We cast concrete not as a surface finish, but as a permanent extension of structural geography. Our cantilevers lean directly over drop-offs where traditional builders dare not step.

Every project is custom formulated with specialized silica fume and basalt fiber matrixes to resist chemical erosion from high-salinity maritime wind. Once poured, a Monolith object is physically married to its underlying geological substrate.

CORE FORMULATION DET-918

Our standard concrete uses Type IX sulphate-resistant Portland cement mixed with locally sourced igneous basalt aggregate, reinforcing structural stability up to 180 MPa tensile thresholds under mechanical shear tests.
