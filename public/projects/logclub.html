// Q & Eww

Questions you probably had.

Does it smell?

Yes. Aggressively. Each pail is double-sealed and foil-lined but we cannot, in good conscience, promise discretion. Open it outside. Open it downwind. Open it after your divorce is finalized.

Are the worms really alive?

At time of packaging, yes. After 38 hours in a USPS truck in July, also probably yes — but rougher. Our 30-day Worm Vitality Guarantee covers any unit arriving with fewer than 50% wiggle-positive specimens.

Can I gift a subscription?

You absolutely can, and we strongly encourage it as a passive-aggressive holiday gesture. We ship with a hand-lettered card that says "I was thinking of you."

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes. One click, no upsell, no guilt. We'll send a tasteful unsubscribe card on heavy cotton stock with no smell whatsoever. We respect the journey.
