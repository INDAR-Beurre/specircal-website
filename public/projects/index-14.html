Our Philosophy

Embrace
the Gloop

"We looked at fine dining and asked: what if it was worse? Not in quality — in aesthetics. What if the food was beautiful but looked like it belonged in a garden… for the wrong reasons."

At CACAux, every dish is a conversation between craft and discomfort. Our chocolatiers spent 18 months perfecting the exact shade of brown that triggers both appetite and existential dread. The result? Three Michelin stars and a restraining order from the FDA's visual standards department.
